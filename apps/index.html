Bench

Philosophy

Here you will find a bank of software that is carefully thought-through.

These are intentionally biased toward being dumb, single-focused software applications for maximum usefulness.

There is a beauty to behold in an object that does one thing well enough.

As its usefulness is proven through continued use, the meaningful incremental improvements to make tend to reveal themselves organically.

That may not be the end-all-be-all, but I believe this approach makes for purposeful craft.
