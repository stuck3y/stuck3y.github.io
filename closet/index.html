Gaming Closet Hideaway Project

Jon's custom gaming space project in a repurposed closet

Project Overview

I'm converting a closet into a cozy gaming retreat while maintaining its storage functionality. This dual-purpose space will serve as both a comfortable gaming area and clothes storage.

Space Specifications

  • Closet dimensions: 72" width x 40.75" depth with 10' ceilings
  • Shelf height: 39" from floor
  • Doorway: 30" wide, centered
  • Existing ventilation (no fan needed)

Key Features

  • Space-efficient design that preserves closet functionality
  • Ergonomic setup for comfortable gaming sessions
  • Integrated lighting system with motion activation
  • Quick transition between gaming and productivity modes
  • Sound isolation to minimize noise transfer to adjacent rooms

Setup Plan Details

  • Monitor Setup: 34" LG monitor wall-mounted on left wall with adjustable mount
  • Seating: Low-profile gaming chair facing left wall for maximum leg room (for 6' tall person)
  • Input: Lap desk for comfortable keyboard/mouse use
  • Tech Integration: MacBook dock at entrance for quick setup, HDMI switch to toggle between MacBook and PS5
  • Storage: Mini fridge and snack cabinet in right corner
  • Lighting: Philips Hue light strips with HomeKit motion sensor for automatic activation
  • Organization: Illuminated floating shelf for controllers and headset
  • Flooring: Plush area rug for comfort
  • Access: Maintained central walkway to access hanging clothes
  • Soundproofing: Acoustic solutions to minimize gaming chatter from disturbing sleeping family members
