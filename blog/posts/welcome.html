A quiet place to write

May 11, 2026 Zen read →

This is the first post on a small blog I've quietly added to my site. I wanted somewhere unhurried to put down longer thoughts — about the small tools I'm building, the things I'm reading, and the workouts and routines I keep coming back to.

The design is intentional. Cream paper, warm ink, a single column you can read end-to-end without tabbing out. Inspired by my Zen Reader — the same instinct: take the noise out, leave the words in.

What I expect to publish here

  • Notes on the little apps I build for myself.
  • Things I've learned the hard way that might save someone an afternoon.
  • The occasional book or paper that genuinely changed how I think.

No publication schedule. No newsletter. Just a place I can write without ceremony.

The first draft of anything is the rough sketch. The second draft is where the real work begins.
